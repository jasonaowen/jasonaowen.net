Title: x230 Keys
Date: 2013-05-23
Tags: hacking
Category: hacking

The Lenovo Thinkpad x230 keyboard is missing a few keys, but they are availble
as unmarked function-key combinations. You can find them in the manual, but
for quick reference, here they are:

```
Break: Fn-b
Pause: Fn-p
SysRq: Fn-s
Scroll lock: Fn-k
```
